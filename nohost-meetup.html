No-Host Meetup Admin Portal

by 10x Software LLC

What this app does

The No-Host Meetup Admin Portal helps group organizers automate routine tasks: scheduling events, sending periodic polls, fetching RSVP counts, and sending small thank-you gift cards to early RSVPs. This tool is for authorized organizers only and is not a consumer application.

Who can access it?

Access is limited to verified admins of our managed groups. End users/members do not log in here. If you are a member, please use your group’s normal event pages.

Data access & purpose

  • Group & event metadata – to plan and publish events.
  • RSVP counts & basic member info – to measure interest and trigger organizer actions.
  • Contact (email) – used only to send organizer polls/notifications when configured.

See our Privacy & Data Use for details on scope, retention, and deletion.

Security & privacy

  • OAuth 2.0 is used for authorization; sensitive tokens are stored in secure server-side vaults.
  • No sale of personal data. Limited retention aligned to admin workflows.
  • Data deletion on request; see instructions below.
