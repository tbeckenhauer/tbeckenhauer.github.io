Privacy & Data Use

Applies to the No-Host Meetup Admin Portal (admin-only) operated by 10x Software LLC.

What we access

  • Group & event metadata (names, times, descriptions)
  • RSVP counts and limited member identifiers necessary for organizer actions
  • Email addresses (when provided/authorized) to send organizer polls/notifications

Why we access it

To automate organizer workflows: scheduling events, sending polls, monitoring RSVP levels, and issuing small gift-card thank-yous to early RSVPs.

How we use & retain data

  • Data is used only for the admin features above; we do not sell personal data.
  • We keep operational logs and minimal state needed to perform tasks; older logs are regularly pruned.
  • Gift-card recipients may have basic payout metadata retained for accounting/compliance.

Security

  • OAuth 2.0 authorization; secrets are stored server-side, not in client pages.
  • Transport over HTTPS only.
  • Production access limited to authorized admins.

Your choices

  • No consumer login: Members do not sign into this portal.
  • Opt-out: Members can opt out of organizer emails sent via this tool.
  • Access/Deletion: Email privacy@soft-wa.re to request data access or deletion. Provide your group and profile details so we can verify identity with the organizer.
